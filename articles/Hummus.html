Hummus

May 30, 2020

This hummus is my go-to item to bring for a potluck. It’s super easy to do and damn tasty.

Step 0: Ingredients

  • 2 cans of drained chickpeas (close to 0.5kg)
  • 1 tablespoon of tahini
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of Homemade Turkish Yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of your favorite fresh herb {thyme, basil, mint, oregano} leaves
  • 4 cloves of Garlic
  • Juice of a half lemon
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of *smoked paprika*
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper or any hot pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning.

Step 1: Blend Everything

So simple, mix well everything in the blender. It should be pinkish orange I am trying to say “yavruagzi” in English here. Too sour? add few teaspoons of tahini or olive oil. Too bland? double down on spices. Too tahini-ish? add few teaspoon of yogurt or juice of a lemon. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste.
